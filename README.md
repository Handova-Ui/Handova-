# Handova
## 1. Project Overview
Handova is a mobile app that connects artisans with customers who love handmade products.
The app focuses on authenticity and craft culture and unique storytelling for every piece.
Handova supports two main user types:
•	Artisan (Seller)
•	Customer (Buyer)
The app also offers events, workshops, and trips related to handmade crafts.
## 2. Main Categories
Handmade products are organized into four main sections:
### Home Décor
Includes candles, pottery, woodwork, macramé, table décor, and more.
### Textile & Fabric Crafts
Includes embroidery, crochet, knitting, patchwork, and handmade fabrics.
### Handcrafted Accessories
Includes jewelry, leather crafts, bead accessories, and metal pieces.
### Art & Paintings
Includes sketches, oil paintings, abstract art, and calligraphy.

## 3. Authentication System
Users can sign up or log in using:
•	Email
•	Phone Number
There are two account types:
Artisan Account: For sellers uploading and managing handmade products.
Customer Account: For buyers browsing products, joining events, and purchasing items.

## 7. Why Handova Has No Chat Feature
Handova does not include chat to protect privacy and keep all communication inside the platform.
This prevents price negotiation, spam, personal contact sharing and keeps a professional environment.
